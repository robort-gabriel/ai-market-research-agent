# Market Research Report Template

Fill this in and write it to `Content/Market-Research/<niche-slug>/<run_date>.md`.

```markdown
# Market Research Report — <niche> — <run_date>

## Recap
<3-5 sentence plain-language recap of the state of the niche this run: what competitors are doing, what's trending, what's newly launched.>

## Recommended Opportunities
1. **<Opportunity title>** (priority: high/medium/low — <N> supporting signals)
   <1-3 sentences describing the opportunity and the concrete action it suggests.>
   - Supporting signal: <competitor move / trend / news item, with source>
   - Supporting signal: <competitor move / trend / news item, with source>

2. **<Opportunity title>** (priority: ... — <N> supporting signals)
   ...

## Sources
- Market scan: `Content/Market-Research/<niche-slug>/<run_date>/market-scan.md`
- Trends & news: `Content/Market-Research/<niche-slug>/<run_date>/trends-news.md`
```

Ranking rule: an opportunity backed by 2+ independent signals (a competitor move AND a trend, or 2+ separate news items) ranks above one backed by a single signal. Never assign "high" priority to a single-signal opportunity.
